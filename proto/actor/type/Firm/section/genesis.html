1998 March 15, 1998
Stuttgart, Baden-Württemberg, Germany

Acme Industries emerged during a pivotal transformation in the European automotive supply chain. Hans Mueller and Friedrich Schmidt, veterans of Bosch's powertrain division, recognized an opportunity to establish a pure-play precision machining company focused on the premium German automotive segment.
